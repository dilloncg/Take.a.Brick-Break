Take a Brick/Break

Welcome to our Brick Gallery! Our Gallery is made up of student work. Each image is 7" x 2" and is a jpeg or a png. The size fits in the bricks and makes them more interesting. It works as a distraction from studying and creates a game. With each gallery there are QR codes that brought you here. I hope you enjoyed the journey! You can submit your own work to dilloncg@vcu.edu, and we'll add it to our gallery! Now Go ahead and look around...
